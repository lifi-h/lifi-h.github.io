💻 About
Unicode is a universal character encoding standard that assigns a code to every character and symbol in every language in the world. As technologies and the world around us grows, Unicode also evolves and adapted to the changes.

This chapter focuses on how the Unicode evolves from time to time that reflects the world around us - from the baroque style, the basic present style where we are at today, and the prediction of the next 2 period of time that is much more blocky and pixelated which we don't understand currently and yet, we will know and understand what it means by the time we get on to that period of time.
